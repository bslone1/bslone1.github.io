B. Slone Student Bio

Bridget Slone "Slone" (she/they) is a researcher, songwriter, and novice data scientist based out of Chicago, IL. Her research primarily combines the fields of music, audiology, and neuroscience. Slone’s songwriting is vulnerable and evolving, and she’s excited to share her debut EP sometime in 2022. Slone is passionate about collaborating across her wide range of interests.

Slone holds a BA in Music and Communication Sciences & Disorders from The College of Wooster, a Master’s degree in Digital Musics from Dartmouth College, and is currently completing a data analytics and visualization bootcamp at Northwestern University.
